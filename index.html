How to Use Fuel Optimizer
  1. 1Plan both legs assuming no tankering — fuel load for each leg is only what you need for that leg.
  2. 2Enter those fuel requirements along with fuel prices for both locations, and fees and waiver amounts for stop B.
  3. 3Press Calculate — the output will be an optimized fueling strategy for your trip.
  4. 4Adjust your tanker leg fuel load based on the strategy and update your plan in ForeFlight.
The model applies an estimated weight penalty of 0.005 gal/hr/lb of extra fuel carried. This is a reasonable approximation for making smart tankering decisions across most aircraft types.
